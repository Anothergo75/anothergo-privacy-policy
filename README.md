========================================
AnotherGo Privacy Policy
Last Updated: February 3, 2026
========================================

This Privacy Policy explains how AnotherGo (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, 
and protects personal data when you use the AnotherGo mobile application and related 
marketplace services (the “App”).

AnotherGo acts as a data controller for personal data processed through the App.


----------------------------------------
1. Scope
----------------------------------------
This policy applies to:
- App users
- Marketplace purchases and transactions
- Merchant order fulfillment
- Customer support
- Marketing communications (where enabled)


----------------------------------------
2. Legal Basis for Processing
----------------------------------------
We process personal data based on:

a) Contract performance (to deliver the service)
b) Legal obligations (financial/tax compliance)
c) Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, service improvement)
d) Consent where required (e.g., marketing and location access)


----------------------------------------
3. Personal Data We Collect
----------------------------------------

A) Information you provide:
- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- Encrypted account credentials
- Optional profile data
- Purchase/transaction information

B) Automatically collected data:
- Device and technical data (device type, OS, IP address, identifiers)
- Usage data (feature usage and interaction logs)

C) Location data:
- Collected only with your permission
- Only while using the App
- Used to show nearby merchants and offers


----------------------------------------
4. How We Use Data
----------------------------------------
We use personal data to:

- Operate the marketplace
- Process purchases and payments
- Enable order fulfillment
- Provide customer support
- Send service notifications
- Send marketing communications (if enabled)
- Prevent fraud
- Improve reliability and performance


----------------------------------------
5. Transaction History Storage
----------------------------------------
We store transaction history to:

- Manage merchant payouts
- Maintain financial records
- Provide users with order history
- Meet legal and tax obligations

Transaction data may be retained for legally required retention periods.


----------------------------------------
6. Data Sharing
----------------------------------------
We DO NOT sell personal data.

Merchants receive user data only:
- When a purchase is made from their store
- Only what is necessary to fulfill the order
  (name, contact details, order details)

We may share data with:
- Hosting providers
- Analytics services
- Infrastructure partners
- Payment processors

All partners operate under contractual data protection obligations.

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect:
- User safety
- Platform integrity


----------------------------------------
7. Payments
----------------------------------------
Payments are processed by regulated third-party providers, including:

- Stripe
- Apple App Store
- Google Play

AnotherGo does NOT store full payment card details.


----------------------------------------
8. Analytics
----------------------------------------
We use analytics tools (e.g., Firebase or equivalent) to:

- Understand app usage
- Improve features
- Enhance stability and performance


----------------------------------------
9. International Transfers
----------------------------------------
If personal data is transferred outside your country/region,
we use appropriate safeguards such as:

- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- Other lawful transfer mechanisms


----------------------------------------
10. Data Retention
----------------------------------------
We retain personal data only as long as necessary for:

- Service delivery
- Legal compliance
- Financial reporting
- Dispute resolution
- Fraud prevention


----------------------------------------
11. Account Deletion
----------------------------------------
You may request deletion:

Inside the App:
Settings → Delete Account

Or via email:
info@another-go.com

Deletion is processed within 30 days unless retention is legally required.


----------------------------------------
12. Your Rights
----------------------------------------
Depending on your jurisdiction (including GDPR regions), you may request:

- Access
- Correction
- Deletion
- Restriction of processing
- Data portability
- Withdrawal of consent

Submit requests to:
info@another-go.com


----------------------------------------
13. Permissions
----------------------------------------
The App may request permissions such as:

- Location → nearby shops/offers
- Camera/Photos → profile photo or QR
- Storage → performance caching

Permissions can be managed in device settings.


----------------------------------------
14. Children’s Privacy
----------------------------------------
AnotherGo is not intended for children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.


----------------------------------------
15. Security
----------------------------------------
We use industry-standard safeguards, including:

- Encryption
- Access controls
- Monitoring

No system is completely secure, but we continuously improve protections.


----------------------------------------
16. Changes to This Policy
----------------------------------------
We may update this policy from time to time.
Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date.
